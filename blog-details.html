AWARDDS AND APPRECIATION

William Blama

-

Jan 19, 2026

Heartfelt Appreciation!

Today, I want to sincerely express my deepest gratitude and appreciation to every single person who has supported, encouraged, prayed for, and believed in me throughout this journey. My heart is truly full of joy, and I do not take any of your love and support for granted. Every call, message, recommendation, encouragement, contribution, and act of kindness has played a huge role in helping me achieve these awards and milestones.

First and foremost, I give all glory to God Almighty for His grace, favor, wisdom, and strength. Without Him, none of this would have been possible. I also want to thank everyone around me — my friends, colleagues, customers, partners, mentors, family members, and the amazing RetailPoint team — for standing by me consistently. Your support has been overwhelming, and I remain forever grateful.

These awards are not mine alone. They belong to all of us collectively because every achievement recorded is a reflection of teamwork, dedication, loyalty, trust, and unity. Each one of you contributed directly or indirectly to this success story, and I celebrate you all wholeheartedly. Thank you for believing in the vision and for helping us continue to grow beyond expectations.

RetailPoint has come this far because of the commitment and effort of amazing people who continue to push the vision forward every single day. I strongly believe that this is only the beginning of greater things ahead. May God continue to bless every one of you abundantly, reward your labor, enlarge your coast, and grant you success in all you do.

My prayer is that RetailPoint will continue to grow from strength to strength, from glory to glory, and from success to greater success, in Jesus’ name (IJN). Together, through unity, love, hard work, and consistency, we shall continue to achieve bigger milestones and make greater impact.

As a little token of appreciation and love, I will be showing some love to everyone in the office tomorrow. 😊 It may be small, but it comes from a grateful heart filled with appreciation for all you do. Thank you for being part of this beautiful journey and for making the work environment a place filled with support, friendship, positivity, and growth.

Once again, thank you all so much for everything. I truly appreciate and celebrate every one of you. May God continue to uplift and bless you all richly.

With love and gratitude,

#Banya Peter
